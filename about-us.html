About Myself

I'm Stefanie Darmayanti, a certified financial consultant specialising in cross-border financial planning. With over 13 years of experience, I help Indonesian expatriates and global professionals optimise their finances, from international tax strategies to investments that suit their risk profile.

My background started in the banking industry before transitioning to independent consulting. I have certifications and often speak at financial seminars, both nationally and internationally.

My approach is practical and solution-based. Through 1-on-1 consulting services, workshops and educational content, I guide clients to:

  • Minimise cross-border tax burden
  • Build a global investment portfolio
  • Achieve financial freedom at a young age

Out of work, I enjoy exploring the latest in fintech and sharing insights through podcasts/blogs. Connect at @stainmoylv or explore my services at www.StainGroup.com.
